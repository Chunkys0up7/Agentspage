Simple Reflex Agent

Beginner

Like a thermostat: "If room is cold, turn on heat." It reacts to what's happening right now using simple rules.

Memory: None - lives in the moment
Best for: Quick, simple decisions
üü¢

Model-Based Agent

Intermediate

Like a personal assistant who remembers your conversation. It keeps track of context to make smarter decisions.

Memory: Remembers recent context
Best for: Customer support, chatbots
üéØ

Goal-Based Agent

Intermediate

Like a GPS navigator: knows where you want to go and figures out the steps to get there.

Memory: Tracks progress toward goals
Best for: Task planning, navigation

Utility-Based Agent

Advanced

Like choosing the best flight: weighs multiple factors (price, time, comfort) to find the optimal choice.

Memory: Evaluates trade-offs
Best for: Decision optimization
üìà

Learning Agent

Advanced

Like a student who gets better with practice: learns from feedback and improves its performance over time.

Memory: Learns from experience
Best for: Personalization, recommendations
üìö

Knowledge Retrieval Agent

Advanced

Like a librarian who finds relevant books and summarizes them: searches through information to answer questions accurately.

Memory: Accesses knowledge bases
Best for: Q&A, research assistance

Multi-Agent System

Expert

Like a team working together: multiple specialized agents collaborate, each handling what they do best.
